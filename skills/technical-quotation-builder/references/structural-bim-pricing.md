# Structural/BIM Pricing Patterns

Use these patterns for structural engineering, BIM, and rebar-related quotations.

## Common Units

- `TON`: detailed rebar detailing/despiece, where effort scales with processed steel tonnage.
- `m2`: estimating by ratios when effort scales with building area or evaluated area.
- `GLB`: pilots, limited fixed scopes, validation cases, or closed deliverables.
- `month`, `day`, or resource formulas: internal support sheets or transparent cost backup.

## Rebar Detailing vs Ratio Estimation

Keep these scopes separate when the user asks for separate acapites:

- Ratio estimation: area or volumetric logic, indicators such as kg/m2, no detailed bar-by-bar despiece.
- Despiece/detailing: detailed rebar development, quantities derived from detailing, consolidation and deliverables.

Do not describe ratio estimation as a subtask under despiece if it has its own row or acapite.

## Pilot Work

Treat pilot work as a validation case when:

- it covers only one building or element
- it was already developed or partially validated
- it has a commercial discount or special fixed price

Keep the pilot separate from the scaled package unless the user explicitly says to include it.
